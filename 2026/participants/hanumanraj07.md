## Projects I've Built

### 🔍 FuelGuard — AI-Based Fuel Fraud Detection
An AI-powered web app that detects fuel fraud from uploaded fueling videos. It analyzes video frames to identify manipulation and generates instant legal PDF reports as evidence. The platform maps fraud hotspots and supports pump ratings and fleet tracking — bringing transparency to fuel transactions.

> **Stack:** Full Stack + AI/ML + PDF generation

---

### 🛒 Warm Bazaar — POS & Inventory for Kirana Shops
A full-stack Point-of-Sale and inventory management system built specifically for local kirana (grocery) shops. Handles billing, stock management, customer credit (udhar), and supplier payments in one platform. Includes real-time inventory tracking, profit analysis, smart reorder suggestions, dashboards, alerts, and lightweight AI insights.

> **Stack:** MERN Stack + AI insights + Dashboard

---

### 📊 Developer Profile Aggregator — Unified Dev Dashboard
A MERN-based platform that aggregates developer stats from GitHub, LeetCode, YouTube, and Sololearn into a single unified dashboard. Features role-based authentication, profile verification, a custom Dev Score, leaderboard rankings, and admin management — helping students and developers showcase their activity in one place.

> **Stack:** MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js + OAuth integrations
