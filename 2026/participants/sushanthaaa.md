# Sushanth Purushothama

## Introduction

I'm Sushanth Purushothama, a PhD Computer Science student at Asia University in Taiwan and full-time IT Engineer Intern at Pou Chen Group (a global manufacturing company).

Over the past 8 months I led the complete replacement of a 7-year-old Joomla CMS with a production Wagtail EIP for our Taiwan headquarters. The project involved full i18n (English + Traditional Chinese with clean URL prefixes), enterprise SSO via Keycloak + django-allauth (including a custom global logout adapter), RBAC with page-level privacy, a complete frontend migration from Bootstrap 5 to Tailwind CSS 3.4 + Alpine.js, and Docker/Kubernetes-ready deployment. I also wrote all the phase reports, SOPs, and architecture documentation that the team now uses.

Given my hands-on experience scaling Wagtail for a large organization, I’d love to help evolve bakerydemo into a more realistic enterprise showcase. My **first preference** is the Demo website redesign project (issue #566), where I can contribute to new sections that test large content volumes, document-heavy structures, and multilingual/RTL readiness while keeping the site clean and maintainable.

My **second preference** is wagtail-localize improvements, where my production i18n work (URL prefixes, custom menu tags, LocaleMiddleware) can directly help the translation workflow.

Since my next internship phase is AI integration (Chatbot + AI title/content assistance using the MCP tool I already built), I can also explore adding a lightweight, realistic “Ask Wagtail” chatbot feature as a bonus demonstration of extensibility but only if that fits the overall vision.
