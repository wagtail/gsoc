# Philip Narteh

## Introduction

Hi everyone! I'm Philip Narteh, an early-career software engineer from Accra, Ghana.

My open-source journey started with Django. I'm a contributor with an accessibility focus and completed the Djangonaut Space programme. Wagtail came onto my radar properly during an open-source internship at Quansight, where I worked on migrating the PyOpenSci website from Jekyll to Django/Wagtail. That project gave me a real feel for how Wagtail works in practice, and it stuck.

I'm interested in the Multilingual support improvements project. Reading through the pain points discussion (#13693), the locale-after-creation problem and the content comparison UI gaps stand out to me, and I'm looking forward to contributing.
