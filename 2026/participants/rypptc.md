## Introduction

Hi! I'm Rodrigo, a web developer based in Alicante, Spain. I work mainly with Python, Django, and Wagtail CMS. I'm also an economist and a translator, which is part of why multilingual content management is an area I care about personally and professionally.

I'm applying for the Multilingual Support Improvements project, with a focus on performance and scalability of core translation operations in wagtail-localize.

I recently participated in the Outreachy application process, which was my first experience contributing to open source. I've been working with Wagtail professionally for several years and I'm already active in the Wagtail Slack community.
