# Samya Aggarwal

I’m Samya Aggarwal, a student developer with experience in Python, Django, and full-stack web development. I’m interested in Wagtail because it bridges the gap between creativity and technology, enabling non-developers to take full control of digital content without relying on code.

For GSoC 2026, I’m especially interested in the Starter Kit Upgrade direction. My current focus is understanding how Wagtail’s starter experience can be improved for first-time users ,especially around page structure, media support and editorial clarity.

Recently, I explored the Wagtail starter by setting it up locally, studying its page types and templates, and adapting it into a more beginner-friendly blog starter.

## Current focus

Right now, I’m mainly exploring the Starter Kit Upgrade idea. I’m especially interested in how Wagtail’s starter experience can be made easier for beginners through clearer page types, better editorial defaults, improved demo content, and more intuitive content structure.
