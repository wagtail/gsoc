# De Wolf

## Introduction

Hi everyone! I'm De Wolf, a Computer Science student from New Delhi, India.

My journey into web development started right after high school when I first learned Python and Django from CodeWithHarry. One of the first projects I built was a small website that checked grammar in text. It was a simple project, but seeing something I built actually work was exciting and pushed me deeper into programming.

As my interest in coding grew, I decided to pursue Computer Science. Later, my brother introduced me to Flask, and I built a few small projects with it. Working with Flask helped me better understand backend development and how web applications are structured.

Recently I’ve been exploring Django and Wagtail more seriously. I'm interested in understanding how large open-source projects are structured and maintained. I’ve already started contributing to Wagtail and opened a few pull requests while learning the codebase and contribution workflow.

I'm excited to continue learning from the community and contribute to Wagtail through GSoC 2026.
